LittleBubs.in Return Policy

We gladly accept returns of new and unused items purchased through our LittleBubs website within 30 days of the original purchase date.

Items labeled as "FINAL SALE" are not eligible for a refund – please refer to the "Product Description" section on each individual product page for details. Returns of unused items in their original packaging will receive a full refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days, excluding original shipping fees.

To initiate a return and request a shipping label, please reach out to our customer service team at customer-service@littlebubs.in. If the return is related to a cosmetic defect, kindly include a photo with your email. For items purchased at a retailer or on Amazon, please contact the original point of purchase for a refund or replacement.

Gift Return Policy

If you've received an item as a gift and wish to return it, you may do so within 60 days of the original purchase. You will receive a website credit to use on a future LittleBubs order. All sale items are final sale, and no returns will be honored.

Shipping Information

Free shipping is applied to all orders totaling ₹1000 or more after any applicable discounts are applied. Orders below ₹1000 will incur a flat shipping fee at checkout. LittleBubs.in currently ships only within India.
